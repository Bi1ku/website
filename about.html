Myself

Want to get to know more about me? The developer behind this travesty of a website? Sure, here you go: my family, background, etc. My whole life story!

I'm Owen Shi, a current sophomore at Stuyvesant High School, interested in STEM. I was born in Queens, New York, on April 13th, 2008, and raised by a traditional Chinese household. At 5, I moved to Greensboro, North Carolina, where I went to elemetary school (shoutout to Claxton Elemetary!). There, I lived for 6 years, helping my parents in our family restaurant and playing video games--enjoying life.

My old childhood home in North Carolina. The gray car is my grandfather's.

However, due to parental conflicts, I moved back to New York City during my 5th-grade summer, attending I.S. 5, Walter H. Crowley Middle School, a school in Queens. I was a bit of a loner, but I made a few friends. I was also a bit of a troublemaker, but a decent student. During the pandemic, which happened at the end of my 6th-grade year, I began to learn to program, which quite literally changed my life. 8th grade went smoothly and I went to Townsend Harris High School before transferring to Stuyvesant due to their superior STEM programs. my favorite classes are Annual Introduction to Computer Science and Physics right now. I'm currently really enjoying my time here, and it's been a great experience!

I live in Woodside, Queens, with my mother and grandmother in a small house. My aunt and cousins live down the block, so it's really convenient. Though I do miss Greensboro's large houses and flourishing nature, I love New York's hustle culture and mentality which motivate me to work harder each and every day!
